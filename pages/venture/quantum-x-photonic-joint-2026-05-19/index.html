Where Qubits Touch Cells

The structural parallel is the institutional joint. The Uruguay Quantum Hub letter to Presidencia names pharma as one of five priority verticals. Photonic Lab is the LATAM substrate where that pharma vertical materializes.

// QUANTUM LAYER

Quantum-assisted candidate selection feeds the Photonic Lab Tramo 1 bioreactor (10–50L). Process data and analytical readouts (HPLC, qPCR, NanoDrop, ELISA) flow back into the quantum-classical hybrid loop for the next iteration. Both halves live in Uruguay; neither half ships abroad mid-cycle.
// PHOTONIC LAB LAYER
Multi-platform GMP-ready bioproduction · single-use architecture · 10–50L Tramo 1

The joint is not "quantum and biotech are interesting buzzwords." The joint is that a recombinant protein candidate triaged on a quantum simulation in Buenos Aires or Uruguay, then produced in a Tramo 1 single-use bioreactor in Photonic Lab, then characterized in the same facility's analytical suite, is a regional deep-tech loop that does not currently exist in LATAM. BOTH operates both halves.
