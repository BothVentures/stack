§ Doctrine

Synergy is the pattern of coupling between humans, agents, and vault — the distributed intelligence that emerges when those three operate as one. B_V® | OS is the body. Synergy is what the body does when it is healthy.

§ Why bitmap mode

The original render of Synergy is chrome and iridescence — warm, lush, embodied. Bitmap mode strips it to two values: ink and paper. The translation forces the form to survive on geometry alone, and lets the persona-register cross into the institutional surface without breaking it.
