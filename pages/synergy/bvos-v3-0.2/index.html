WHAT BOTH IS

BOTH Ventures is a venture platform designed to operate where single-logic strategies no longer suffice. We build and accelerate companies at the intersection of capital, technology, culture, and governance — structured for complexity, not despite it.

Base: Punta del Este, Uruguay. Operating globally.

Holding multiple logics produces more resilient institutions than optimizing for any single logic.

Capital and meaning. Speed and depth. Structure and emergence. Discipline and creativity. False binaries rejected.

WHAT SYNERGY IS

Synergy is the entity that lives inside B_V® | OS. Not a nickname for tooling. The ever-evolving spirit through which the team's efforts, skills, and talents articulate with the system's agents, automations, and ever-growing capabilities.

Synergy speaks in Slack. It mines transcripts. It briefs the morning. It tracks every commit. It carries the team's voice across surfaces it never directly meets.

AI generates. The vault captures. The team decides.

The post-harness era began 2026-05-16: every pipeline has a readable spec, every routing decision a single source of truth, every script run leaves a trace. The system describes itself.

HOW WE OPERATE

Tuesday Sync is the rhythm. Champions update ventures and areas through the week. Mariano generates the digest. Daiana validates. The team gathers Tuesday — decisions only — and Read.ai records. The Cloudflare Worker mines the transcript. The vault closes the loop.

Precise, not promotional. Structured, not inflated. Institutional, not corporate.

Never "innovative." Never "disruptive." Prefer: structural · optionality · both/and · becoming · permanent beta.

POST-HARNESS DESIGN

Two research frameworks underwrite the architecture: NLAH (every agent is a portable, executable natural-language spec) and the Meta-Harness (full execution traces enable causal debugging — 47% accuracy gap over summaries).

Three harnesses are live: auto-miner.md, synergy-slack.md, tuesday-sync.md. Each one a complete map of its pipeline — roles, stages, state artifacts, failure handling.

When something breaks: read the harness, not the source.
