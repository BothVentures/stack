02 — The Place

The place is the moat.

Most events rent a city. This one is built on one. The regulatory clarity and the capital are already here — Punta del Este simply gives them three days to converge. The Southern Cone's Biarritz, with a stronger regulatory frame and a larger capital pool.

01 — Regulation
A clear digital-asset frame
Law 20,345 gives Uruguay a defined digital-asset framework, and the Central Bank (BCU) is active on oversight.
02 — Capital
Where the region's money summers
The highest concentration of HNW individuals and family offices in LATAM through the Q4–Q1 season. Argentina, Brazil, Chile and Uruguay all operate here.
03 — Stability
First in LATAM for rule of law
Uruguay ranks first in the region for political stability, rule of law and anti-corruption. USD contracts are standard.
04 — Brand
A premium signal that travels
“Punta del Este” carries the same premium signal across LATAM that “Dubai” carries globally.
