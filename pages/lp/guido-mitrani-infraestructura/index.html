Platform — Synergy & Operating System.

StageOperational

Reported before the positions because the institutional case for BOTH as a multi-position platform depends materially on this layer being live and durable. Not a position to evaluate alongside the ventures — the operating layer that makes the multi-position case credible at current team size. Owned and operated by Mariano Boris.

Synergy is the connective tissue between the team (5 principals), the work product (the vault — every decision, risk, position status, transcript), and the toolset (Cloudflare Worker, Supabase with 12-table schema and pgvector embeddings, GitHub, Notion, Asana, Slack, Anthropic API for inference). It runs three pipelines today: (i) an auto-mining pipeline (Read.ai webhook → Worker → vault + Asana + Slack DM to champion) that converts every recorded meeting into structured decisions, risks, and action items; (ii) a daily-brief pipeline that surfaces priorities per principal each morning, pulling from calendar, mail, Asana, and vault; (iii) a weekly digest pipeline for the Tuesday Sync, the firm-wide decision-making cadence.

The strategic point: a multi-position platform — four substantive positions follow on the next pages, with more in the pipeline — is operationally indefensible at this team size without an OS layer that absorbs the coordination cost. Synergy is the reason a five-person firm runs the portfolio that follows without execution drift. It is the operational moat, not a P&L line.
