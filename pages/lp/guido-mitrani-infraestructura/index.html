Photonic Labs — deep-tech research platform.

StageExplore · Build trigger

Unified deep-tech platform converging three pillars on a single physical substrate (photonics): bioproduction (Pillar 01), quantum R&D (Pillar 02), and AI compute (Pillar 03). Convergence formalized 12 May; public narrative V0.2.0 locked 19 May. Recognized internally as one of three BOTH Ventures flagship positions alongside Reshore and Casa Manantial.

The platform's commercial surface presents four offerings: (i) a multi-platform bioproduction facility (GMP-ready, 10–50 L pilot, ~ 60–70% of regional biotech pipeline addressable without retooling); (ii) integrated computational capabilities (analytical compute, process modeling, candidate design); (iii) innovation & development services; and (iv) a regulatory + institutional layer anchored in ANII / Zona Franca / Decreto 329/025 (in force February 2026) / Ley 19.566.

The economic case for Pillar 01 (biotech) is stress-tested in an April-2026 financial model: CAPEX midpoint USD 2.1 M (range USD 1.52–2.72 M with 30% contingency), ARR break-even USD 420 k, cash break-even Month 22 (base case), gross margin 72% on Equipment-as-a-Service, 68% on GMP project services. Model is viable on Streams 01–03 without equity participation in residents.
