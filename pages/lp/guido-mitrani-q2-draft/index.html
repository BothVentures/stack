Platform Operating Layer — Synergy.

StageOperational

Reported before the positions because the institutional case for BOTH as a multi-position platform depends materially on this layer being live and durable. Not a venture to evaluate alongside the others — the operating infrastructure that lets BOTH sustain a four-position book with the process discipline a multi-position fund typically requires multiple times the team size to maintain.

Synergy is a three-tier coordination system. A knowledge layer — every firm meeting recorded, transcribed, and run through an auto-mining pipeline that converts unstructured conversation into structured decisions, risks, and action items, each routed to the right owner and the right venture. A memory layer — a living institutional knowledge base (the "vault"), every position, decision and risk versioned and searchable. An action layer — daily briefs to each principal pulling from calendar, mail, task queue and vault; the weekly partners' meeting runs against a pre-generated digest.

A multi-position platform with four positions and more in pipeline needs a layer that absorbs the coordination cost. Synergy is that layer — what lets BOTH run the portfolio without execution drift.

Quarter contextQ2 milestone: the layer is now running continuously across all positions and used by the team as the default operating system.
Strategic surfaceBeing positioned as institutional intellectual property in its own right. Sandbox-first, security audit pre go-live. If executed, Synergy becomes a separately-valuable asset.
Cross-portfolio linkThis document was drafted on the platform. Each section was prepared against vault pages the system maintains. The reporting cadence is itself a Synergy deliverable.
Position postureHeld outside the LP commitment. Funded as platform operating expenditure (USD ~650 per month at current load).

3.4 · b
Side quest — Quantum.
Launch windowQ3 — Q4 2026
Side quest of 3.4ExploreHands-on

The quantum compute direction of Photonic Labs, structured as a sovereign-aligned Uruguay hub through BOTH's working partnership with The Quantum Alliance (TQA, exclusive IonQ LatAm partner). The thesis is straightforward: Latin America does not have an integrated quantum-AI compute hub today; less than 1% of global quantum investment reaches the region; the institutional window to position a country as the regional anchor is open and time-limited.

The opportunity. TQA is building the first distributed advanced-computing network in Latin America — quantum hardware plus AI compute, deployed locally under sovereign control. Buenos Aires is the first central node. Uruguay is the next: structurally suited (stable rule of law, mature multilateral relationships, existing institutional framework for science and innovation) and timed to a market entering its infrastructure deployment phase. The global quantum market is projected at USD 97B by 2035 (McKinsey). The window for foundational positioning closes once anchor hubs are placed.

The partnership being built. Working term sheet drafted in Q2 between BOTH and TQA. Two-entity structure: a new Uruguay-domiciled operating entity (NewCo Uruguay) is set up to acquire and operate a 6th-generation 256-qubit IonQ trapped-ion system, with the hardware financed through Uruguayan State plus multilateral (BID-track) instruments. In parallel, BOTH receives a 5% equity stake in TQA HoldCo via an equity swap. National contraprestación designed in: training of approximately 1,000 Uruguayan students and technicians. Non-binding until definitive agreements executed.

Q2 progress with the State. Institutional letter to Presidencia ROU prepared with TQA. Technical reports delivered to support the institutional dialogue, covering financing structures (BID, multilaterals, sovereign vs. non-sovereign vs. blended/PPP). Engagement underway with the relevant Uruguayan agencies (ANII, Uruguay XXI, academic partners) ahead of the public announcement window.

EndgameUruguay positioned as the regional quantum-AI hub. BOTH holds ownership in the local infrastructure asset and an equity position in the broader network.
Position postureQuantum hardware financing routed through State and multilateral instruments. BOTH carries economics through ownership in the NewCo Uruguay operating entity and the TQA HoldCo stake (5%). Q3 critical-path: definitive legal execution and confirmation of the multilateral financing track.
