Welcome to Both_Ventures.com!

An agentic venture platform · Punta del Este, Uruguay · Online since 1998* · *and forever becoming.

Hello and welcome to the official homepage of Both_Ventures — a brand-new institutional kind for the post-Shift world. We're not a fund. We're not a studio. We're not an accelerator. We're not a holding company. We're not even an AI lab. We're a venture platform whose operating substrate is itself intelligence — human and artificial, held as one system.

"Both_Ventures is an agentic venture platform — a shared operating intelligence, human and artificial, that builds, accelerates, and interconnects deep-tech ventures reshaping infrastructure for a post-Shift world, where single-logic strategies no longer suffice."

Take a moment to explore our site. Above, our flagship Synergy applet renders the living yin-yang of our operating intelligence — bits and q-bits in continuous orchestration. Move your mouse over it! Below, you'll find our doctrine, our portfolio of nine deep-tech ventures, and information on how to reach our team in Punta del Este.

iAbout Us

Bits and q-bits to transform the atom. That's our phrase. Digital and post-classical computation, applied to physical reality — water, materials, biology, energy, supply chains, governance rails. We call this work "the new code-order": the world that emerges when infrastructure is re-coded by institutions designed with intelligence at the core rather than at the edge.

We operate from Manantial Casa in Punta del Este, Uruguay. We work globally — Latin America, Europe, the Middle East, North America. We're proudly in permanent beta. (That means: it is both what it is and what it is becoming.)

DThe Doctrine — Our Philosophy

Contradiction is not an error within systems. At some times, it is the main motor. We're built to hold tensions — not balance them, not eliminate them. Orchestrate them.
